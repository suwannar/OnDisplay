OnDisplay
People give up privacy for attention and admiration.
The Concept
OnDisplay examines our obsession with sharing. Today, technology has made it easier than ever for us to share images, thoughts and things. The Information is being shared so fast that we need to work harder to maintain privacy. Our desire to be popular leads us to post on public forums.
